ICT4D Story

The geospatial tool is being developed using an Information and Communications Technology for Development (ICT4D) approach, outlined in Bon et al (2016), in collaboration with stakeholders in Fiji and Tonga.

The ICT4D approach presents a collaborative software development methodology that emphasises:

  • inclusion of different stakeholder groups
  • addressing issues of sustainability of ICT use
  • encouraging contributions from stakeholders at each design phase
  • aligning with and supporting stakeholder needs
  • building on existing software
  • developing an ICT to have immediate relevance to current work processes
