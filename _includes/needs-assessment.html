Needs Assessment

Identify unmet needs for geospatial data and applications

Focus groups in Fiji and Tonga with landscape stakeholders to identify unmet needs for geospatial data or GIS software. Expressed needs were documented and prioritised based on cost, technical feasibility, data availability, user capacity, and scalability.

This prioritisation of needs provided focus for tool design and contributed to a process of aligning ICT with stakeholder requirements.

Priority needs:

  • crop and farm survey mapping apps
  • land use and land cover dynamics maps
  • climate and weather information

Extension officers, or people in similar roles in other organisations, were identified as key end-users.
