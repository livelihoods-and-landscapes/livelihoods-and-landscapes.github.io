Geospatial applications for climate-smart landscape management

Using a collaborative information and communications technology for development (ICT4D) process, we are developing open-source mobile, desktop, and cloud tools to monitor and map diverse landscapes and seascapes.

This project is funded by the Australian Centre for International Agricultural Research (ACIAR) and is a collaboration between stakeholders in Fiji, Tonga, Australia, and New Zealand. We are testing and developing geospatial applications in diverse landscapes and ecosystems across Fiji and Tonga.

  • map landscape resources in the field using mobile GIS
  • monitor inter-annual landscape dynamics using satellite images
  • capture rich socio-economic and agricultural information
  • spatial dashboards to integrate field data with secondary geospatial data layers
  • spatial databases and server-side applications to manage spatial and non-spatial datasets
